Background

Ashleigh Hollowell is a journalist who is going to do some cool investigative reporting one day. She received her master's degree from Syracuse University and her bachelor's degree from CSU-Pueblo in Colorado. Ashleigh is a native of a town of 98 people, Guffey, CO, but now lives in San Diego, California.
